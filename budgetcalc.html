Budget Calculator (February-March)

We collaborated with EECU in which they wanted a budget calculator that would benefit graduate students and young adults.

Tools Used

  • Figma
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • Javascript/Chart.js

Experience

This project was almost a full class work collaboration. We were all assigned to work on each other’s budget calculators until the due time. Before starting, we went through the competitive audit of what websites had that helped other clients. From my research, there was a lot of simple designs and information that helped clients and users understand how budgeting works. Taking their designs to my idea, I used Figma to create an easy access budget calculator– and after getting my prototype finished I was able to tell my classmates how I wanted my calculator to be.

Challenges

The biggest challenge was making the dropdown menus interactive through Javascript. Over time I was able to debug some minor issues by reading and understanding the Javascript and adjusted the dropdown logic so it could work properly.

Habits of Mind

  • Collaboration
  • Communication
  • Critical thinking

Outcome & Reflecting

This project helped strengthen my confidence in Javascript and debugging. It also taught me well about collaborating with unique people for this workflow’s improved design before working on the development on my own.
